From the pharmaceutical manufacturer's loading dock to the patient's medicine cabinet — and the biohazard bin back out — we handle every step so your staff can focus on patients.

Flagship service

Built for retirement and long-term care.

Retirement homes, nursing homes, and group homes face a daily challenge: getting the right medication to the right resident at the right time, every time. Our complete LTC service combines McKesson's PACMED NS pouch packaging with hands-on delivery support to make medication administration safer and simpler.

Personalized compliance packaging

Each resident's medications are sorted into individual time-of-day pouches, labeled with name, date, and dose.

Barcoded bedside scanning

Every pouch carries a standardized barcode for verification at the point of administration — reducing dispensing errors.

Scheduled facility delivery

Weekly, bi-weekly, or custom delivery cycles to match your facility's medication review schedule.

Staff training & support

We train your care team on the pouch system and provide 24/7 support for emergency medication needs.

Biohazard & sharps

Certified disposal for medical waste.

From single-pharmacy sharps containers to high-volume hospital and lab pickups, we handle the certified collection, transport, and disposal of biohazardous waste — keeping your facility safe, compliant, and audit-ready.

Sharps & needle containers

Scheduled pickup of full sharps containers and supply of fresh containers — no more compliance gaps between visits.

TDG-compliant transport

All transport meets Transportation of Dangerous Goods regulations, with trained personnel and proper documentation.

Chain-of-custody documentation

Every pickup logged, every disposal certificated — full paper trail for inspections and audits.

Pharmaceutical waste

Expired and unused pharmaceuticals collected separately, with destruction documentation provided.

Pharmacy automation

McKesson-powered. Pharmacist-trusted.

Cold chain

Temperature-controlled, end-to-end.

Vaccines, biologics, insulin, and specialty pharmaceuticals demand strict temperature control. We deliver temperature-controlled transport from 2°C to 8°C refrigeration, plus frozen and ultra-cold options for specialty medications.

Validated packaging

Pre-qualified shippers maintain target temperatures for 48–96 hours, even in remote transit.

Continuous temperature monitoring

Real-time data loggers with full transit records — every shipment gets a temperature certificate.

Multiple temperature ranges

Refrigerated (2–8°C), frozen (-20°C), and ultra-cold (-70°C) capabilities for specialty biologics.
