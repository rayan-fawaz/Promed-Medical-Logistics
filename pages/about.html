Our story

From an Ottawa clinic
to a national supply chain.

Promed Medical Logistics is the logistics arm of the Promed group — built on a decade of front-line pharmacy and clinical experience, expanded into a Canada-wide medical supply chain partner.

Who we are

Pharmacy DNA. Logistics scale.

Promed started as a community clinic and pharmacy in Ottawa — caring for families, running medication delivery routes, packing compliance pouches for retirement homes, and learning every day how complex healthcare logistics really is.

What we learned: hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, and care facilities across Canada need a logistics partner who actually understands medicine. Who knows what a controlled substance manifest looks like. Who can pack a cold-chain shipper at 2°C and tell you exactly when it'll arrive in Iqaluit.

Promed Medical Logistics is built for that. Pharmacy-grade attention to detail combined with logistics-grade national reach. 62,000+ sq ft of warehouse capacity across five distribution hubs from Ottawa to Edmonton, trusted carrier partnerships with Canada Post and UPS, and a team that understands medication safety the way only a healthcare-native operation can.

What we believe

Three principles that shape every shipment.

01

Medicine first, logistics second.

We started in pharmacy and clinic operations, and that's the lens we bring to everything. Every shipment is a patient — not a parcel.

02

Compliance isn't optional.

MDEL licensing, TDG certification, controlled-substance protocols, chain-of-custody — we get the regulatory side right, because the alternative is unacceptable.

03

National reach, local care.

We've scaled to every postal code in Canada — but our team still answers the phone, knows your facility by name, and shows up when it matters.
